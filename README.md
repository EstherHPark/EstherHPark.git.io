### Tableau - San Francisco Average Income and Medical Marijuana Dispensaries by Zipcode

San Francisco has recently legalized recreational marijuana and since, the number of dispenaries have rapidly increased. Medical marijuana dispenaries location was collected from Data.gov and median household income data was collected from zipatlas.com. It can be seen that marijuana dispensaries targeted neighborhoods with high median income. This information is important because it shows that recreational activities in San Francisco are changing and business such as these target upper class residents.
